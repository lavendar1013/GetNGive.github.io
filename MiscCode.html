American International Group
American International Group is a leading insurance company in the United States who is a strong supporter of charities and various forms of volunteer work. For every AIG employee who contributes $5,000 or more to a non-profit organization, the company will double the amount given.

One Laptop per Child
One Laptop per Child is a nonprofit organization that provides children in developing nations with special low-cost, low-power laptops that encourage learning opportunities otherwise inaccessible to them. Donations of any amount can be made on their website to benefit the future generations with self-empowered learning.

The Company Store
Since 2015, The Company Store has affiliated with the non-profit organization, Family Promise, to bring warmth to children who do not have access to this and other luxuries alike. For every comforter that it sells, it will donate another one to those in need during the winter.

Out of Print Clothing
Out of Print Clothing is a quirky online store for clothing, paper goods, and accessories featuring a classic piece of literature. With the help of its partner, Books for Africa, it promises to donate a book to a community in need for every product sold.

Better World Books
Better World Books is an online shopping platform meant for purchasing and selling textbooks and books under their mission to provide everyone with opportunities for literacy. As a result, they are committed to matching every purchase on their website with a book donation to someone in need with their partner: Book for Book.

Warby Parker
Warby Parker is an eyewear retailer that sells trendy eyewear at reasonable prices on and offline. In addition through their partner, VisionSpring, they promise to donate a pair of eyeglasses to a person in need for every product they sell.

Burt's Bees
Burt's Bees is a popular retailer that sells all natural lip products. In addition, through their Greater Good Foundation, they donate a portion of their earnings to foundations such as Habitat for Humanity, the Boys and Girls Club, Alliance Medical Ministry, and Farmer Foodshare. Through this, they help to provide shelter, food, and other necessities to those in need.

Africa on the Ball
Africa on the Ball is a nonprofit organization that uses athletics to bring healthcare, education, and enterprise to children living in developing nations in Africa. In addition to providing children with footballs to encourage teamwork, they are also hoping to educate them on the importance of leadership through lessons on being a role model and setting and acheiving goals.
