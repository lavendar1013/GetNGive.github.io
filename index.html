"Give a little get a little"

Welcome

Welcome to Get n' Give! This site hopes to link consumers to companies, businesses, and websites which use their profits to give back. Using Get n' Give you can shop for a cause by giving back through your purchases.

Purpose

People struggle with finding time to give back to their communities. Get n' Give allows you to give back to your community while helping yourself.

About Us

Melanie, Paris, Ileanna and Ruby's love for social good motivated them to make a website that made giving back easier.
